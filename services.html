My services

My tailored coaching sessions will provide a safe space, using a holistic approach to:

  • Explore areas of your life that you may be feeling stuck in.
  • Provide support and guidance to set new goals and get what you want in your life.
  • Rediscover your dreams that may have got buried along your journey.
  • Provide accountability to help you move towards your desired goal, whatever that may be.
  • Focus on yourself and the areas in your life that may need some balance.

In addition to all of the above, my coaching sessions will provide family carers and parents that have gone through or who are going through a diagnosis for their child with:

  • Acknowledgement, empathy and support.
  • Guidance on moving forward
  • Acceptance and rediscovering their individual self.
  • How to navigate this new journey that they involuntarily find themselves on.
  • Coming to terms with being both a carer and a parent to your child.
  • Practical information on where they may be able to access services and support.

I really look forward to providing all of the above support in 2023. Until then please feel free to follow me on LinkedIn,Facebook and Instagram for thoughts, inspiration, guidance and support.
