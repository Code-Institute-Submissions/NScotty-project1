About Me

I am a mum to three children and carer to my youngest daughter Ellie. Last year, after being out of the workplace for over ten years, I knew that I needed to start doing something for myself again, outside my caring role. I had always worked from a young age and missed both the interaction and independence that the working role brings. My confidence had diminished during my time out of work and I really wasnâ€™t sure exactly what I wanted to do.


Fast forward one year. I successfully completed a back to work course specifically for Family Carers. The course provided support around the practicalities of going back to work but also offered coaching and personal support around lack of confidence and concerns around returning to work. I availed of both counselling which I had done before and coaching which was new to me. The experience of coaching was life changing. For the first time in years I had a goal to work on for me but also gained a realisation of /insight into how beneficial the years in my caring role would be to me going forward and how all of the strengths and skills I had gained are invaluable, lived experiences. I knew going forward that I possessed a true empathy for what people are going through in their lives, especially those in a caring role.


Along with my caring role, I have been working as a part-time Project-Assistant since January 2022, on the same course that I completed myself. An opportunity that opened up for me to work with two cohorts of amazing family carers, supporting and guiding them to the next chapter of their journey, confirmed for me that coaching was the next chapter of my journey. I felt that having both the lived experience of being a carer and also having been a previous participant on the course I could support the groups with pure empathy and truly acknowledge their struggles. So I have taken the steps to put a professional hat on all of my experiences in life both personally and professionally to become a Life Coach. I am hoping that with some hard work (and a bit of good luck) that I will be qualified to begin this journey in June 2023.

My Approach

I will provide a safe space, for you the client, to learn how to look inwards and explore areas in your life where you may be feeling lost or stuck. While I will support you in this process you will decide on the goals you want to achieve and the steps that you want to take to get there.


I am particularly passionate about supporting Family Carers and parents that have gone through or are going through a diagnosis for their child/children. Having gone through this myself, I know how frightening and overwhelming it feels to be handed a piece of paper with a diagnosis on it for your child and then sent on your way with no idea of how to navigate this new information or deal with the huge emotions that are unearthed.


I will provide support and guidance around this very delicate time and offer supports and signposting on the practical side of things going forward. Although every diagnosis is not the same, the paths of which will cross along the way.
